Order Jari & Taika to entertain at your party

Jari and Taika are a full-service dance orchestra of five nice musicians that bring momentum and entertainment to your party. We are doing gigs everywhere in Southern and Western Finland from Humppila. From extensive software there are, for example, evergreen jazz classics over the years, finland favorites, daytime screams and a few of their own compositions. We have a rhythm in the blood and entertain the audience as a matter of heart.
